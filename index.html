Our Services

Getting started

  • We begin with a free 20-minute intake meeting where we answer your questions and explain our process and services.
  • We start with an in-depth review of your medical history and current status.
  • We work with you and your family to understand your options and wishes.
  • We help communicate your choices to your team.

At the hospital

  • We continuously track your medical status with daily medical chart reviews and team communication.
  • We make sure your treatment plans are being followed.
  • We visit you in person at the hospital as needed and communicate with your team.
  • We attend family meetings and help resolve conflicts when those arise.

Before discharge

  • We ensure your discharge plan is safe and aligned with your goals.
  • We research and advise on next steps - at home or in a facility.
  • We connect you with our network of specialists to support your recovery.
  • We stay actively involved until you safely transition to the next level of care with the services you need.
