What I do for you and your family

Getting started

  • A free 20-minute intake to explain your needs and ask questions
  • Share relevant medical history and current status
  • Understand your options
  • Get acquainted with the process of working together

At the hospital, you get -

  • In-person hospital presence as needed
  • Daily medical chart review
  • Treatment plan and status tracking
  • Communication of your concernes and wishes to the medical teams
  • Family meeting facilitation and conflict resolution

Before discharge, I -

  • Confirm the discharge plan is safe and aligned with your goals
  • Research and advise on next steps - at home or in a facility
  • Connect with my network of top specialists
  • Ensure seamless transition to the next level of care
