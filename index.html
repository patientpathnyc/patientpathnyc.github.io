What I do for you and your family

Getting started

  • A free 20-minute intake to answer questions and explain my services
  • Review your medical history and current status.
  • Understand your options and wishes.
  • Communicate your choices to your medical team.

At the hospital

  • In person hospital presence as needed.
  • Daily medical chart review and team communicatzion.
  • Treatment plan and status tracking.
  • Family meeting attendence and conflict resolution.

Before discharge

  • Confirm discharge plan is safe and aligned with your goals.
  • Research and advise on next steps — at home or in a facility.
  • Connect with my network of specialists.
  • Safe transition to the next level of care.

Specilizations and clinical experience

Drawn from a decade at the bedside, in emergency departments, ICUs, step-downs, and rehabilitation.

Neurological

  • Stroke / CVA
  • Traumatic brain injury (TBI)
  • Brain tumor, resection, craniotomy
  • Guillain-Barré, Myasthenia Gravis
  • Aphasia, agraphia, alexia
  • Cognitive-communication deficits

Neurodegenerative

  • Dementia, Alzheimer's disease
  • Parkinson's disease
  • Multiple Sclerosis

Cardio-thoracic & respiratory

  • Acute respiratory failure (intubation, tracheostomy, ventilation)
  • COPD
  • AAA repair, CABG
  • Organ transplant (lungs, heart, liver)

Swallowing & airway

  • Dysphagia, swallowing impairment
  • Vocal fold paralysis / paresis, glottic insufficiency
  • Feeding tubes (NG, PEG)
  • Esophageal cancer, esophagectomy, Zenker's diverticulum

Spine & surgical

  • Anterior Cervical Discectomy & Fusion (ACDF)
  • Post-surgical inpatient recovery

End of life

  • End-stage liver disease
  • End-of-life care planning & coordination
