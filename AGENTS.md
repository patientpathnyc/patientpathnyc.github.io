# Patient Path NYC - AI Agent Guide

## About This Site

Patient Path NYC provides professional patient advocacy services for hospitalized patients in New York City. Founded by Shlomit Liberty, MS, MA, CCC-SLP, BCPA, a Board Certified Independent Patient Advocate with over a decade of healthcare experience.

## Contact Information

- **Phone:** (917) 283-2765
- **Email:** shlomit@patientpathnyc.com
- **Website:** https://www.patientpathnyc.com
- **Location:** New York City area (Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, The Bronx, Nassau County, Westchester County, South Fairfield County CT)

## Services Offered

1. **Initial Consultation** - Free 20-minute intake meeting
2. **Hospital Care Management** - Daily medical chart reviews, team communication, in-person visits
3. **Discharge Planning** - Safe discharge plans, facility research, specialist connections

## Site Structure

### Main Pages
- `/` - Homepage with services, about, and contact information
- `/faq.html` - Frequently asked questions about patient advocacy

### Resource Pages (Educational Content)

#### Hospital Preparation
- `/navigating-hospital-stay.html` - Essential packing guide and tips for hospital stays
- `/pack-for-hospital-stay.html` - Complete hospital bag checklist
- `/medical-history-form.html` - Form to organize medical history and contacts

#### During Hospital Stay
- `/communicate-symptoms.html` - STARS Strategy for describing symptoms
  - **S**pecific Location
  - **T**reatment Tried
  - **A**ssociated Symptoms
  - **R**elief Factors
  - **S**everity Level
- `/fight-hospital-infections.html` - Prevention practices for hospital-acquired infections
  - Statistics: 2M patients/year affected, 1/31 patients, 100K deaths annually
  - Practices: Oral care, masks, hand hygiene, surface disinfection, reporting warning signs

#### Discharge Planning
- `/safe-discharge.html` - Guide to safe hospital discharge
  - Levels of care: Acute rehab, sub-acute rehab, LTACH, home health, outpatient
  - CUS Tool for self-advocacy: Concerned, Uncomfortable, Safety
